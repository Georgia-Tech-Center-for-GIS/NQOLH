NPU V

Geography

NPU V is in the Intown South area of Atlanta and includes Adair Park, Capitol Gateway, Mechanicsville, Peoplestown, Pittsburgh, and Summerhill.

Demographics

Sources: (City of Atlanta 2010 Census Summary Report, Neighborhood Planning Unit V ); Neighborhood Quality of Life and Health Indicators Project

Landmarks & Institutions

A few of the landmarks in NPU V include Adair Park, Pittman Park, and the Turner Field baseball stadium.
