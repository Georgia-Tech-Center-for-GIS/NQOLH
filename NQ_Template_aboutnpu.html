NPU A

Geography

NPU A is located on the Northside and includes the following neighborhoods: Chastain Park, Kingswood, Margaret Mitchell, Mt. Paran Parkway, Mt. Paran/Northside, Paces, Pleasant Hill, Randall Mill, Tuxedo Park, West Paces, Ferry/Northside, and Whitewater Creek.

Sources: (City of Atlanta 2010 Census Summary Report, Neighborhood Planning Unit A ); Neighborhood Quality of Life and Health Indicators Project

Landmarks & Institutions

NPU A is known for having Chastain Park, the largest park in the city, as well as the West Paces Ferry classical houses. It also has a concentration of private primary and high schools, including Westminster, Lovett, Pace, Galloway, and Trinity.
